Methodology

For both Linear Regression and ARIMA models we used next parameters to forecast next day closing price:

  • Sentiment score of news
  • Log Return (ln(Closing Price) - ln(Prev day Closing Price))
  • Trading Volume
  • Overnight Variation (Day opening - Last day closing price)
  • Trading Day Variation (Day high - low price)
  • Closing Price of previous day
For linear regression model we used this formula to calculate next day closing price:

{#
#}

\(ClosingPrice_{t} = \beta _{0}\\ + \beta _{1}*SentScore_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{2}*LogReturn_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{3}*TradingVolume_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{4}*OvernightVariation_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{5}*TradingDayVariation_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{6}*TotalNumberOfWords_{t-1} \\ + \beta _{7}*ClosingPrice_{t-1} + \varepsilon _{t}\)

For comparison we used ARIMA(0,1,4) model with closing price as a parameter.
Both models were trained on 80% of data and then tested on 20% of data to calculate accuracy and value loss (RMSE) of each one. (Price and news dataset from 2016-01-01)
